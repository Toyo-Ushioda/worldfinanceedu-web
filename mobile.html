03 Practice 03 of 03 · Investment
Beyond training and CFO services

WFE also invests in startups.

WFE invests its own capital directly into early-stage startups at pre-seed and seed stage. To date, WFE has backed 10+ startups directly and holds stakes in 2 VC funds covering 40+ startups. Each investment is personal and conviction-driven, focused on the founding team and the problem they are solving.

  • 01
    Pre-seed and seed only
    Stage focus is pre-seed and seed, the points where founder selection, network and judgement matter most relative to capital.
  • 02
    Sector-agnostic, founder-led
    Sector is open. The decision is driven by the founding team and the problem they are solving, not by category preferences.
  • 03
    Post-investment support
    Startups backed by WFE get access to WFE's finance and operating experience, separate from any paid engagement.
